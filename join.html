Join Our Team

We are always interested in motivated and curious scientists who share our passion for antimicrobial drug discovery.

PhD Students

We welcome applications from enthusiastic students interested in joining our lab through national or international doctoral fellowship programs. Please contact us well in advance of the application deadlines to discuss potential projects and funding opportunities.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We are keen to host postdoctoral researchers with a background in structural biology, biophysics, or chemical biology. If you are considering applying for individual postdoctoral fellowships (e.g. Marie Skłodowska-Curie, Juan de la Cierva, EMBO), please get in touch to explore possibilities.

Master’s and Undergraduate Students

We regularly supervise MSc and undergraduate research projects. If you are a student in chemistry, biochemistry, or related fields and are interested in gaining experience in structural and mechanistic enzymology, contact us to discuss available opportunities.
