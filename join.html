Join Our Team

We are always interested in motivated and curious scientists who share our passion for antimicrobial drug discovery.

🔬 PhD Students

We welcome applications from students with backgrounds in microbiology, biochemistry, pharmacology, bioinformatics or related fields who wish to pursue a PhD in our lab. Interested candidates are encouraged to contact Dr. Laura Díaz Sáez directly to discuss potential projects and funding opportunities. Prospective PhD students could also explore national and international fellowships such as FPU, FPI, or Marie Skłodowska-Curie programs.

🧫 Postdoctoral Researchers

We are happy to support applications from postdoctoral researchers seeking independent fellowships. If you are interested in joining us, please get in touch early to plan a proposal and identify suitable funding sources (e.g., Marie Curie, Juan de la Cierva, EMBO).

👩‍🔬 MSc and Undergraduate Students

We offer research placements for motivated MSc and undergraduate students who wish to gain hands-on experience in antimicrobial research. Students can undertake thesis projects, laboratory training, or internships depending on current opportunities.

📩 How to Apply

If you are interested in joining our group, please email laura.diazsaez@youruniversity.edu with the following:

  • Brief cover letter explaining your research interests and motivation
  • CV (including academic record and publications, if applicable)

We strive to create a supportive, inclusive, and interdisciplinary environment where every member can contribute to advancing antimicrobial drug discovery.
